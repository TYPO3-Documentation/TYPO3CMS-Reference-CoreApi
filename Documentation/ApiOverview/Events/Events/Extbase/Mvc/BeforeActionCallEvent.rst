=====================
BeforeActionCallEvent
=====================

The PSR-14 event :php:`\TYPO3\CMS\Extbase\Event\Mvc\BeforeActionCallEvent` is
triggered before any Extbase action is called within the :php:`ActionController`
or one of its subclasses.
